Services

Let's Get Started

30 minutes, Free

I would come and see the space that we want to work with. Listening to your dreams and goals of what you would like to do and gather information to get you a better estimate.

Take Me Shopping

45+ minutes, Free

Not sure what you want to put in your space? I love our local nurseries and would be more than happy to help you hand pick the plant babies that you want. I would guide you in the direction of what would blossom and thrive in your space. I have a bunch of places to suggest to go and find these treasures too!

Full Garden Plan

Duration varies, $100

This would entail a full service of planning, testing the soil, choosing plants, and making a blueprint of the space. With a week time frame to come up with the best solution for your dream garden. This will be the start of your yard's transformation!

General Upkeep

2 hours, $30

This would either be a weekly or biweekly 1 hour visit to maintain your garden. Weeding, watering, and overall care.

Weeding/Clearing

1+ hour, starts at $30

Creeping Charlie and Crabgrass got you down? I'll get those garden beds up to snuff. Let the backyard fill with leaves? There I am with a rake and garden bags. Starting at $30 for the first hour and $15 for any additional hours.

Tilling

3.5+ hours, starts at $50

Tilling a space takes work and grit. A typical 4x4 hand tilled garden bed starts at $50 and usually takes 3-4 hours. Larger plots that would need a machine tiller and extra muscle (AKA my husband Pat) starts at $100 and goes by an $15 hourly rate. This includes uprooting grasses and plants to clear the plot.

Pruning

45+ minutes, starts at $20

Trees and bushes need hair cuts too! Starting at $20 for smaller (6ft and under) shrubs and trees. Up to $40 for larger trees (6ft-12ft). I cannot do anything with a ladder to to legality reasons, so I would have to be able to reach it with the telescopic pruner.

Planting

Duration and price vary

From seeds to shrubs and trees. Some are more labor intensive than others. Price varies due to duration, quantity and size of plant/garden bed.
